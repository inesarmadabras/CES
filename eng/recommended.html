MySNS Recommended Apps

MySNS Recommended Apps is our opportunity to share mobile applications developed by citizens and other organizations that, in our view, play a significant role in bringing citizens closer to their health.
The content and performance of the recommended applications are entirely the responsibility of the manufacturer.
